# MarketingGenius AI – AI-Powered Marketing Kit Generator for Small & Local Businesses

One-click full-stack Generative AI SaaS that instantly creates professional marketing content for small business owners — no prompt engineering required.

### What it does in 5 seconds:

- 3 ready-to-post Facebook posts
- 3 emotional Instagram captions with CTA
- 3 Google Ads (headlines + descriptions)
- SEO keywords & hashtags
- Brand tone guide + conversion copy
- All perfectly tailored to the business

### Tech Stack (Full-Stack GenAI Application)

- Backend: Python FastAPI + Google Gemini 2.5 Flash
- Frontend: React 19 + Vite + Tailwind CSS
- AI: Gemini structured output
